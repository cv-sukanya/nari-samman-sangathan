about us

Creating a Safe, Equal, and Just Society for Every Woman

Nari Samman Sanghathan is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to empowering and uplifting vulnerable communities across India. Our work primarily focuses on supporting women at risk of gender-based violence, destitute and elderly women, widows, and migrant workers along with their families living in challenging urban environments. With compassion and purpose, we strive to create a safer, more equitable society by offering protection, advocacy, and resources to those who need it most. At the heart of our mission lies a deep commitment to dignity, justice, and the holistic well-being of every individual we serve.

our mission

Our Mission to Serve, Love, and Grow

Our mission is to provide protection, support, and opportunities for rehabilitation through grassroots programs, awareness campaigns, and sustainable development initiatives.
By focusing on women's rights, urban poverty, and migrant welfare, we aim to create lasting change and uplift the lives of those in need. Join us in our journey to build a safer, more inclusive, and empowered India.
