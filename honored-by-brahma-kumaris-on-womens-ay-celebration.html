Honored by Brahma Kumaris on Women’s Day Celebration

On the momentous occasion of International Women’s Day, our Founder, Dr. Sundari Thakur, was deeply honored by the revered spiritual organization Brahma Kumaris during a gathering of over 200 inspiring women leaders, changemakers, and community contributors.

The event was a soulful celebration of womanhood, inner strength, and collective purpose, where the love, blessings, and recognition bestowed upon Dr. Thakur became a powerful reminder of the responsibility that comes with being a voice for others. It served as a motivation to continue her mission of empowering women, uplifting underprivileged communities, and promoting social transformation.

In her heartfelt words, Dr. Thakur shared that this honor belongs not just to her, but to every woman who dares to dream, lead, and bring about meaningful change in society.

We extend our sincere gratitude to Brahma Kumaris for recognizing and supporting the journey of service, compassion, and empowerment.
